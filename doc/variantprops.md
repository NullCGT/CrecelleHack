# Object Properties
- The player may wish for object properties. However, such a
  wish will only be successful if the object in question is
  nonmagical.

## Briny
- Briny armor provides swimming.
- Briny weapons soak their target in water.
- Briny boots spread water.

## Nulling
- Nulling armor provides magic resistance.
- Nulling weapons have a 1/3 chance of cancelling the monster
  they strike.

## Boreal
- Boreal weapons fire ranged projectiles in the form of shards
  of ice. This only happens when the wielder is standing on
  frost or solid ice.
- Boreal weapons deal 1d4 extra cold damage.
- Boreal armor provides cold resistance.
- Boreal boots spread frost when walking.

## Sanguine
- Sanguine weapons absorb blood. If the wielder is standing on
  blood, the blood is absorbed and the attack deals double damage.
- Sanguine boots spread blood when walking.

## Blazing
- Blazing weapons can create a bonfire underneath the target.
- Blazing armor provides fire resistance.
- Blazing boots turn any grass walked on to ash.

## Crackling
- Crackling weapons have a 50% chance of creating a 1d6 damage
  ray of lightning.
- Crackling armor provides shock resistance when worn.

## Acidic
- Acidic weapons deal 1d8 extra acid damage.
- Acidic armor provides acid resistance when worn.
- Acidic boots leave behind a trail of acid when walking.

## Hungry
- Hungry weapons drain life.
- Hungry armor causes increased hunger.
- Hungry boots slurp up blood on the ground.

## Subtle
- Subtle weapons are permanently poisoned.
- Subtle armor provides stealth when worn.

## Hexed
- Hexed weapons have a 100% chance of absorbing curses when uncursed,
  but immediately become cursed upon doing so.
- Hexed armor autocurses when worn.
